The transforms back into their usual selves from being clams, takes a long rest, and levels up twice. The gang goes back to their base and is debating what to do next. Malfendius is drinking and everyoby is planning what to do next.

Luka, Malfendius, and Leor are calling Tourna on the Scrying Mirror to find out if she has any new intel. She reports that her dad took a trip to Icelandale with troops and is annoyed that the gang lost all of the artifacts.

Leor starts singing a lyric poem about a hunter known for biding his time, Hunter Biden. Malfendius is looking around the kitchen for tomatoes to load into his eldritch blaster and shoot, which he shoots at Leor for their bad lyric poem.

A shorter-than-usual halfling (The Librarian) knocks on the door of the base wearing a purple blindfold, we realize he is the warlock from the library. He is confrontational about how the gang fucked up and after arguing, we slam the door in his face. He continues to argue through the door and Luka, Primrose, and Malfendius aggressively argue with him and call him names. He apologizes and says that he is here to help, this is confirmed by Luka after an insight check. Malfendius realizes he is the equivalent of 15 human years old. He uses Misty Step to go through the door and aggressively proposes helping him out. Luka tries to use Sleight of Hand to steal his stuff but he catches Luka.

The Primrose decides to refer to The Librarian as "Pringles, Jr" which pisses him off and everybody laughs at him for a while. He eventually decides to share that his name is Eugene Softfellow, which the gang makes fun of him for even more. Eugene was able to hold onto one of the artifacts from Volp initially, but he lost it and he has decided to help recover the artifacts more now. Eugene explains that he was working in the field and was promoted to middle management but was demoted due to losing the artifact and wants to recover it with the help of the gang.

Another knock happens on the door and an angelic voice asks if she can come in. It's the giant angel that we ran into earlier in the campaign. She glares at Eugene and is annoyed he's here. She is also happy that Alumor is a patron of Hoar The Doombringer, God of Vengence and Retribution.

The Angel explains that Kirby is a primordial God who wishes to bring about all the planes of existence through the cult of Dendar and says that we have to work together.

Everybody bickers even more and Malfendius shoots another tomato at Leor. The Angel threatens to kill herself and we all think this is funny. Primrose agrees to help out as long as the Angel and Eugene distribute bean monsters which they reluctantly agree to do in part of their respective realms. Luka wants to become the God of Murder but is turned down and the Angel proposes allowing Luka to induce fear in anybody within a 30 foot radius (a roll with disadvantage). Malfendius requests some beer and innate spellcasting abilities. Eugene gives Alumor heavy plate armour with +2 in addition to plate armor and resistance to fire damage after she asks for cool armor. Leor is given an accordian which gives +1 to any buffs used by Eugene.'

The Angel tells everyone to hold hands to teleport them to Icelandale. Luka realizes the holding hands isn't necessary so we all teleport even though not everyone is. We get to Icelandale and it's very cold. We look around Icelandale and notice 10 different towns and it's not clear where the Dendar cult is located.
