DM Note: Devan was actually himself but Kirby killed him and wore his skin like a mask

The gang starts fighting Kirby. They are able to eventually push him back into the circle and briefly paralyze him. Luka figures out that he is letting himself get paralyzed because he feels he can get out of the portal if he wanted to and he wants to trap us in it.

He eventually Misty Steps out of it and becomes unparalyzed. The team continues to deal him damage, upsetting Kirby who decides to start attacking more offensively. The gang continues to attack him and eventually his body is literally falling apart, he appears to be a magical creature of some kind but we can't figure out what his deal is exactly.

Eventually he explodes into a pile of meat and fluids but it starts bubbling and a tentacle monster emerges as Kirby says "you've unlocked my second form" to the anger of everybody. Kirby is able to succesfully use Mental Prison on Malfendius trapping him inside of a game of Bean Monsters. Eventually after damaging Tentacle Kirby more, Leor is able to use Dispell Magic to free him.

Primrose uses Summon Woodland Creatures and summons Agdon Longscarf, who apparently haunted Primrose when he was younger (not elaborated further on?).

Kirby casts Mass Polymorph, which affects everybody but Primrose. Everybody is transformed into a clam. Kirby forces all of the artifacts out of our bodies and forces them all to come to him, and he teleports away after saying "see you guys up north".
