Meta note: Alumor’s sword has Prismatic Spray now.

We arrive to Iceland Dale to frightful weather. Janet (the Angel) suggests splitting the party in order to cover more ground. Tourna’s people are on their way to Iceland Dale. Luka grabs Malfendius’ mirror and calls Ezekiel to see if he can help out with scouting for the shards in the towns in Iceland Dale using giant birds but Ezekiel isn’t able to do this, but instead he decides to start his way over to help out in-person.

After Leor accidentally flirts with a child, Luka and Malfendius get on his broom and everyone else flies (due to Malfendius using magic) and we all arrive at Dougan’s hole. Everybody gracefully flies except for Primrose who trips and is flopping through the air. They arrive at Dougan’s Hole and begin asking questions to a local such as “take us to Dougan” and “is this what’s left of his hole” but this leads nowhere and eventually they break off the conversation after finding out that there aren’t any cultists around. Luka feels motivated to save the world so that the villagers and gang can attend FishFest 2022.

Leor is able to use Clairvoyance to surveil a town and the gang considers doing this to make sure the towns are safe to land in. Malfendius writes in the sky "Dendar sucks shit" for any of the cultists but if you’re not a cultist it just says something unnotable like “hi”.

Next we all fly away for 30 minutes to travel to Good Mead. People are friendlier and there are more people around. We all go to the mead hall except for primrose who goes to look at the shrine of the flaming sword. The group enters the mead hall and note an ornate decorative flagon outdoors and a bee raising area. Luka questions a man named John Passerby about if there are any Snake Cultists around and he says there aren’t, who then informs them that they’re having a party with all-you-can-drink mead and Malfendius covers them. Primrose asks the shrine maiden about what the deal is with the flaming sword and the maiden says that the town founder carved it out with a flaming sword; primrose prays at the altar. Back at the hall, Malfendius is playing Edwards Flagonhands and Leor orders a (diluted) light mead after being turned down for an order of an IPA (Icewind Dale Pale Ale) after finding out it’s out of season. Primrose invetigates the altar and doesn’t find anything particularly useful and can’t tell if the magic being used at the altar is anything but benign. They decide to leave.

Luka steals the Mirror out of Malfendius’ pants and everybody sloppy drunk calls Ezekiel. Darkness has fallen over Good Mead and the group rests at a tavern in town. Luka remembers that she has the wand of locate creature and casts it to find her father and figure out the location of the cultists, and it reveals that he isn’t in town. Luka starts drunkenly deliberating over killing her mom and Alumor suggests everybody go to bed instead of dwelling too much on their respective traumas. Malfendius doesn’t sleep so he scowls on the porch. Luka questions Alumor about where the cult of dendar would perform a sacrifice and she remembers that it would probably around the mountain Kelvin’s Cairn where the body of Dendar was buried after an ancient war.

Everybody wakes up and we head towards the city Caer-Konig at the base of the Kelvin’s Cairn mountain. Malfendius is rehydrating with holy water. We see Eugene as we arrive and he thinks its messed up Malfendius is drinking that in front of him. Eugene says that he figured out the cult’s entrance is here and the gang is like yeah we figured that out. Primrose opens the door but Luka asks Leor to open the doors to the Ruined Caer and so Leor falls down and bonks their nose on the ground because they didn’t know the doors were open. The floor moves in a trap door and Luka throws up; Leor pulls her hair back and Malfendius magically cleans up the vomit. The gang walks down the tunnel for several hours and we see a cavern with a strange disc-shaped city. Luka recognizes this as the fabled lost city of Necropolis that was home to an advanced civilization that practiced necromancy; it emanates a green-purple glow that sheds no warmth. Malfendius and Luka note that the necromantic energy of the place would be used for the resurrection of Dendar. Luka also recalls that there’s a plague (Arcane Blight) that killed all of the original dwellers and Malfendius hesitates about entering the city. Malfendius recalls that Arcane Blight first causes paranoia and hallucinations, then clamminess and miopia, then eventually they turn into a monstrous, clammy creature incapable of complex thought.

Eugene wants to go into the city and Malfendius resists it. Luka says fuck it and she walks into the city after everybody deliberated for a while about what to do next. Primrose, Leor, and Alumor also go in. Malfendius hangs outside the city, watching us and afraid. As we walk into the city, we notice two tall spires with gargoyles clinging to it. Primrose reads the rune on the spire and it turns out to be an Insanity Rune among many other runes. Luka checks of the Gargoyle is alive and finds that they can spring to life at any moment.

Malfendius casts Gaseous Form and enters the city, slowly catching up to everyone. Leor and Primrose begins to feel paranoid, indicating the onset of Arcane Blight. Leor wants to go into the entrance of the insanity towers and Alumor suggests that we don’t go into them and push into the city towards the Orb. Luke realizes that she can’t rely on her friends to get through this and elects to sprint through the city; Malfendius hops on his broom and follows and Primrose tags along. Alumor and Eugene elect to stay behind and keep an eye on Leor.

Running gang find a black obilesque with runes on them indicating the 8 schools of magic. Luka casts an apple towards the obilque to see what happens. Between the three at the obilesque, they all cast various schools of magic between them thinking that something would happen. Nothing happens.

Spire gang see 7 imp-ish creatures appear before them and they are about to attack.
